More than 3d?

Illistrations and Concept art are another passion that I regularly explore. I lean more on photoshop to edit designs from shadows and highlights creating somewhat surrealistic works. However my style is not set in stone. I often do design darker content based of off what I imagine but I can still create plenty of bright high energy designs.

A Few Designs I have made in Photoshop

Photoshop is a great tool that I often use to take my concept art to a more finalized version. I also use photoshop to create 2d prints of my 2d artwork. The only shortcoming of the 3d software that I use is the paiting system is not the best but the models can be taken into photoshop where the customization is limitless. I personally find some of the extream contrast of my models to be some of my best 2d artwork.

Fine Art that I have Made

I also paint, sketch, and occassionally create physical 3d froms. I take inspiration from Salvador Dali, Adam Adamowicz, Edvard Munch, Edward Hopper, and many others. Drawing and painting are great art forms that may seem more authentic too certain individuals. I am a still learning new tricks everyday and am glad to be able to continously improve in this hobby and love to take on any project that you would want created.
