Why 3d models?

Three-dimensional art has been around since the dawn of time and when thinking of this ancient marble statues of Greece or Rome may come to mind. This art form brings a sense of presence and tangibility that images cannot always bring. Today thousands of designers can now print photorealistic details, but not many individuals see the possibility to bring amazing personalized creations into their homes! Now seeing this site, you can fill your life with more of the things you love, whatever that might be through this art form!

What are these Used For?

The prints can be ornaments for your home or other decor, or they could be decals on your stuff! These models can be animated through other programs to be used in projects and luckily the program I use is fully compatible with other 3d design applications.

A Few people I have made

One of the things I am best at is facial features and human anatomy. People have very unique characteristics and I always start from scratch for each model. I have created many celebrities, rock stars, everyday people, and historical figures through this program. Creating these kinds of designs can often be very challenging because the amount of detail and slight features that make someone them self are subtle but very noticeable.

A Few Creatures I have Made

I have always loved creating fictional characters for any genre. Demons, Monsters, Aliens; you name it! I typically make my models through a free software called Sculptris available from Pixalogic. I have used this simple and amazing program for several years and is great for a beginner to anyone else. If you have questions or comments, please share your thoughts!
