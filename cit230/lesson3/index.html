Site Purpose:

The purpose of this site is to quickly report the weather of three cities in both a clear but visually intriguing way. The focus of the site is to engage those who are outdoors enthusiast and local families. The artwork is intended to grab the attention of these kinds of individuals. The font color was also chosen for it enthusiastic and vibrant colors to grab the attention of these kinds of people. The site will take the local weather and change the banner, colors, and designs accordingly. It will tell the actual current weather of each location. There will also likely be a community form of places to visit when certain whether is going on.
